Welcome to Bergen.


The moist part of Norway, We proudly present our expertise in clothing for those rainy days. With an average of two hundred and thirty one days of rain every year, we have provided our compatriots wit dry functional clothing for generations and we are hoping to provide dry clothing for the next generations to come. Active people we got you covered!
